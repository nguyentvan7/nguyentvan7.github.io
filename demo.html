ARtifacts Markers

Welcome to the image marker gallery for ARtifacts. If you aren't able to visit a physical ARtifacts showcase, you can use the ARtifacts app on your phone to view the augmented reality exhibits below.

Although it is possible to view the markers on the website, it is recommended to print these markers out and place them at least 3 feet apart.
